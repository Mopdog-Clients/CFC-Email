The recording of our webcast, “Best Practice Considerations for Person-Centered Dementia Care" is now available. Caring for people with dementia can be a delicate matter, but approaching those afflicted in a person-centered way is pivotal to their well-being. It is projected that over 50 million people globally are currently living with dementia, with this number anticipated to surge to 131 million by 2050. As professionals in aging services, it is imperative to adopt evidence-based approaches aimed at improving the quality of life for those under our care. This involves offering comprehensive training and resources to ensure optimal care provision.
If you couldn’t join us for our live webcast, we have you covered! Access the FREE recording today and hear from multiple health care experts about best practice considerations, resources, training tips and practical approaches for person-centered dementia care.
After listening to this episode, aging services professionals will feel better equipped to help their patients navigate life with dementia.

  • Essential elements of the person-centered care approach in dementia care.
