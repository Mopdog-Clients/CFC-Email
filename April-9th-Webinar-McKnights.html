Register Today: FREE CE WEBINAR on Addressing Unnecessary Hospital Readmissions Through Person-Centered Chronic Disease Care
Join us on Thursday, April 9, 2026, at 2:00 PM EST for the complimentary webinar “Addressing Unnecessary Hospital Readmissions Through Person-Centered Chronic Disease Care.”
This webinar explores how person-centered, in-home chronic disease management can significantly reduce readmission risk by addressing clinical, functional, and psychosocial drivers of decline.
Participants will review proven tools, such as INTERACT, evidence-based care transition practices, and ComForCare’s Reducing Hospital Readmissions Program, to strengthen continuity of care, improve early risk identification, and support safer recoveries at home.
This complimentary one-hour session will offer 1 CE credit for Nurses, Case Managers, Social Workers (MN) & NAB for live session attendees.

Session Objectives:

  • Identify common clinical and non-clinical drivers of avoidable hospital readmissions among older adults with chronic disease.
  • Apply person-centered strategies to support disease self-management and early symptom recognition.
  • Describe how tools such as INTERACT enhance communication and escalation across care settings.
  • Explain how in-home care programs can support care transitions and reduce rehospitalization risk.
