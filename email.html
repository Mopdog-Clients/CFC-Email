The shift to value-based care is leading medical professionals to serve seniors in low-cost settings. Thanks to new rules from CMS, non-medical home care is helping them do that.
Starting in 2020, Medicare Advantage (MA) plans can cover services that address social determinants of health for people with chronic diseases. This creates a strategic opportunity for hospitals, insurers and post-acute care providers as they work to improve care and lower costs.
