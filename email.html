FREE Webinar: Strategies for Implementing
Person-Centered Dementia Care
1 CE Credit Available
Join us on Tuesday, June 20, 2023 at 11:00 AM ET for a virtual session focused on person-centered dementia care.
As aging services professionals, it is important to implement evidence-based strategies that will enhance the quality of life of individuals who are under our care and provide training and resources to provide the best care possible.
This complimentary session will offer 1 Nursing CE credit available for live session attendees.

Session Objectives:

  • Define dementia and explain why dementia caregiving is different from other types of caregiving.
  • Describe 3 key aspects of person-centered care philosophy applied in dementia care.
  • Identify 3 strategies and/or additional resources to advance person-centered dementia care in different contexts.
