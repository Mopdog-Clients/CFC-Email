Register Today: FREE CE WEBINAR on the Power of Partnerships in Transforming Care across the Continuum
Join us on Thursday, August 7, 2025, at 2:00 PM ET for the complimentary webinar “The Power of Partnerships: Transforming Care Across the Continuum.”
This insightful session will explore how collaborative relationships can break down silos, improve care transitions, and support better outcomes for aging populations. You will gain practical strategies for building and sustaining partnerships that foster innovation, improve access to services, and drive meaningful change in the lives of older adults.
This complimentary one-hour session will offer 1 CE credit for Nurses, Case Managers, Social Workers (MN) & NAB.

Session Objectives:

  • Define the value and impact of cross-sector partnerships in improving care coordination, reducing fragmentation, and enhancing outcomes for older adults across the continuum of care.
  • Identify key strategies to build and sustain collaborative relationships among health care providers, aging services organizations, and community-based partners.
  • Apply real-world examples of partnership-driven innovations that support person-centered care, reduce duplication of services, and address the holistic needs of older adults.
